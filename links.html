Working of Hyperlinks using <a> Tag

The <a> (anchor) tag is used to create hyperlinks in HTML.
The href attribute specifies the URL of the page the link goes to.
The target attribute specifies where to open the linked document.

Examples:

1. Click here to visit Google (opens in same tab):
Go to Google

2. Click here to visit YouTube (opens in new tab):
Go to YouTube

3. Click here to jump to the bottom of this page:
Go to Bottom




You are now at the bottom of the page!
